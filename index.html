About our cattery

Baltic Bengals is a small cattery focused on temperament, health, and early socialization. Every kitten is raised in-home, receives age-appropriate vet care, and is introduced to daily household life from the start.

What to expect

  • Health screening and documented records
  • Regular updates from birth to go-home day
  • Support on nutrition, transition, and enrichment

Reservation & Bitcoin payment flow

  1. Application review: After your inquiry, we confirm kitten availability and discuss fit.
  2. Reservation invoice: We issue a reservation amount in EUR plus a real-time BTC equivalent.
  3. Wallet confirmation: You receive a one-time Bitcoin address and payment window instructions.
  4. On-chain verification: Once confirmed, your kitten is marked reserved and you receive written confirmation.

Final payment details and handover timing are shared in your reservation agreement.
